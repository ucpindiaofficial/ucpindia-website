Our Manifesto

Ten clear commitments to fix what matters most.

Ten commitments focused on the real problems Indians face every day - and practical solutions to fix them.

1. Quality Education for Every Child

Fix the gaps in our schools - trained teachers, proper infrastructure, modern and digital learning, and skill-based education that prepares students for real jobs. Make quality higher education affordable and accessible to all.

2. Healthcare Within Everyone's Reach

Strong public hospitals and primary health centres, affordable medicines, and quality care in rural and urban areas alike - so no family is pushed into debt by illness.

3. Safety, Dignity and Empowerment of Women

Women are half of India - their safety and success is the nation's success. We will ensure swift, certain justice for crimes against women, safe streets, transport and workplaces, real economic independence through jobs, equal pay and support for women-led enterprises and self-help groups, and strong representation of women in leadership.

4. Children and Youth - Protection and Opportunity

Our children and youth are the nation's future - we will both protect them and open doors for them. For children: safety and protection from abuse and child labour, nutrition, early-childhood care, and safe, quality schools. For youth: world-class skills, real jobs and entrepreneurship, and a genuine voice in public life.

5. Justice and Prosperity for Farmers

The people who feed the nation deserve a fair, assured price for their yield and modern storage that stops waste and lets them sell when prices are right. We will back farmers with reliable irrigation, easy credit, direct market access, and relief from debt.

6. Jobs and Livelihoods for All

Create real jobs close to where people live - through small businesses, startups, services and large-scale skilling that matches people to the work of today and tomorrow.

7. A Self-Reliant India - Built at Home

Back our own makers and industries - support local manufacturing, small and medium enterprises, and energy independence - so we create good jobs at home and depend less on imports.

8. Decentralized Development and Strong Infrastructure

Put funds, powers and decisions in the hands of local bodies and panchayats, and deliver reliable roads, power, clean water, public transport and digital connectivity in every region - not just big cities.

9. Clean, Accountable and Effective Governance

Open budgets and data, digital, paperless and corruption-free processes, whistleblower protection and citizen oversight - with an empowered administration that has clear authority to act and is held strictly accountable for results.

10. Equality, Technology and a Sustainable Future

Equal dignity and opportunity for all regardless of gender, faith, region or background; technology-driven public services; and clean, sustainable growth that protects tomorrow.

Honest Politics. Real Progress.
