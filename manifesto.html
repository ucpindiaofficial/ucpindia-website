Our Manifesto

Ten clear commitments to fix what matters most.

Ten commitments focused on the real problems Indians face every day - and practical solutions to fix them.

1. Quality Education for Every Child

Fix the gaps in our schools - trained teachers, proper infrastructure, modern and digital learning, and skill-based education that prepares students for real jobs. Make quality higher education affordable and accessible to all.

2. Healthcare Within Everyone's Reach

Strong public hospitals and primary health centres, affordable medicines, and quality care in rural and urban areas alike - so no family is pushed into debt by illness.

3. Justice and Prosperity for Farmers

The people who feed the nation deserve a fair, assured price for their yield and modern storage that stops waste and lets them sell when prices are right. We will back farmers with reliable irrigation, easy credit, direct market access, and relief from debt.

4. Jobs and Livelihoods for All

Drive employment through manufacturing, small businesses, startups, and large-scale skilling - creating real opportunities close to where people live.

5. Decentralized Development

Put funds, powers, and decisions in the hands of local bodies and panchayats. Development planned by communities, for communities - with balanced growth across every region, not just big cities.

6. A Self-Reliant India

Build strong domestic industry, local manufacturing, and energy independence - reducing our dependence on imports and making India resilient and self-sustaining.

7. An Empowered, Accountable Executive

Give administrators the clear authority and tools to act and deliver - cutting red tape and delays - while holding them strictly accountable for results and outcomes.

8. World-Class Infrastructure

Reliable roads, power, clean water, public transport, and digital connectivity in every region - the backbone of opportunity and a better quality of life.

9. Transparent, Corruption-Free Governance

Open budgets and data, digital and paperless processes, whistleblower protection, and citizen oversight - so government can be verified, not just trusted.

10. Equality, Technology and a Sustainable Future

Equal dignity and opportunity for all regardless of gender, faith, region or background; technology-driven public services; and clean, sustainable growth that protects tomorrow.
