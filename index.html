United Citizens Party (UCP) is a movement to put people back at the centre of Indian politics.

Our Purpose

Our goal is simple: honest, accountable, and future-focused governance that truly serves the people. We are not about left or right - the development of every citizen is our aim.

What We Stand For

Power to the People

Decisions made with citizens, not behind closed doors. Your voice shapes policy.

Transparent Governance

Open budgets, open data, open decisions - a government you can actually see.

Accountability for Our Actions

We own what we do and the results we deliver. Clear promises, measurable outcomes.

Equality & Development

Equal dignity for all, and progress that reaches every citizen and every region.

Women, Children, Youth & Farmers First

Special priority for their safety, protection, education, jobs and dignity in every policy we make.

Be Part of the Change

Change starts with citizens who refuse to settle. Add your voice to the movement.
