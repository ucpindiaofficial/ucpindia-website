Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions.

What does UCP stand for?

Power to the people, transparent government, accountability for action, and development that reaches everyone.

Are you left-wing or right-wing?

Neither. We judge ideas by whether they help people and the country's future - not by ideology. Our focus is development, people, and the future.

How can I join?

Visit the Join page and fill out the membership form. Membership is free.

How can I volunteer?

Go to the Volunteer page and submit your details.

How is the party funded?

We are committed to transparent funding. Donation and disclosure details will be published as the movement formalises.

Where can I follow updates?

Check the Media page and our official channels for announcements.
