About UCP – United Citizens Party

UCP (ಸಂಘಟಿತ ನಾಗರಿಕರ ಪಕ್ಷ) is a citizens-first platform focused on people’s power, transparent governance, and accountable leadership.

Why UCP?

  • Power to people, not just to positions.
  • Radical transparency in decisions and spending.
  • Clear accountability for promises and performance.
