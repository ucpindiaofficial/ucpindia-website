About Us

Why we exist, and how we are different.

Who We Are

The United Citizens Party (UCP) is a people-driven movement to put people back at the centre of Indian politics.

We believe politics has spent too long arguing about left versus right while everyday problems - jobs, schools, healthcare, clean governance - go unsolved. We choose a different path: development, people, and the future.

Why We Exist

Too many citizens feel unheard, and too many promises go unkept. UCP exists to change that - by making power transparent, leaders accountable, and government genuinely useful in daily life.

How We Are Different

  • Beyond ideology: we judge every idea by whether it helps people, not by which "wing" it belongs to.
  • Evidence over slogans: policies based on data and real results, reviewed in the open.
  • Citizens first: you help set the agenda - not just vote once every five years.

Our Promise

Honesty about what we can do, transparency in how we do it, and accountability for the results.
