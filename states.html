UCP in Karnataka

Karnataka is where our movement began. Here is how we are organized across the state.

State Leadership

Our Karnataka state team coordinates the movement, supports local chapters, and represents members' concerns. Detailed profiles will be published soon.

Local Chapters

We are building district and city chapters across Karnataka - in Bengaluru, Mysuru, Mangaluru, Hubballi-Dharwad, Kalaburagi and beyond. Each chapter organizes citizens around local issues.

Stay Connected

Join our Karnataka community channels for updates, meetings, and volunteering. Email ucpindia.joinus@gmail.com to connect with your nearest chapter.
