UCP Manifesto

This manifesto outlines the principles, commitments, and governance values that guide UCP. It is a citizen‑driven framework focused on transparency, accountability, and participation.

1. Governance Principles

  • Transparent decision‑making processes
  • Open access to public information
  • Clear accountability for public roles
  • Citizen participation in policy discussions

2. Public Service Commitments

  • Ethical leadership standards
  • Responsible use of public resources
  • Community‑focused initiatives
  • Inclusive and accessible governance

3. Citizen Rights & Responsibilities

  • Right to information
  • Right to participate in civic processes
  • Responsibility to engage constructively
  • Responsibility to uphold democratic values

4. Future Vision

UCP aims to build a governance model where citizens and leadership collaborate with clarity, honesty, and shared responsibility.
